Benefits

PPL NETWORKS MEMBERS ADHERE AND COMMIT TO THE FOLLOWING

Honour the rules and regulations set by the network in respect of the usage of the PPL Bill of Lading
Honour the rules and regulations set by any other national or international shipping, forwarding or maritime association

Support each other's business
Reply to all freight inquiries within 24 hours unless otherwise agreed
Reply to all sales leads within 48 hours
Work closely together in a friendly atmosphere respecting each others culture, religion and political situation

For business between PPL Networks members, monthly statements should be provided by the creditor
Settle all debts within thirty days of invoicing one another, preferably via the PPL NETWORKS E-Wallet
Pay their membership fees and any other dues linked to PPL NETWORKS programs or conferences in a timely manner and not later than 15 days after invoice date

Do their utmost to attend the PPL Annual Global Meetings, not missing 2 consecutive meetings without acceptable reason
For members who can't readily attend the AGM, do their utmost to attend the PPL Middle East Meet, not missing 2 consecutive meetings without acceptable reason

Members who are in breach of the rules & regulations, risk suspension or early termination of the membership. No fees will be refunded in such cases.
